Project Description

The Library E-Gate project revolutionizes check-in and check-out procedures at Amrita School of Arts and Science College in Kochi through its intuitive web-based platform. By enabling users to scan their ID cards upon entry and exit, the system seamlessly records attendance data, facilitating efficient tracking of library usage. With its robust functionality and user-friendly interface, the application empowers librarians to generate detailed reports on library activity, empowering the institution with valuable insights for resource allocation and decision-making.

This innovative project not only enhances the efficiency of library operations but also improves the overall experience for students and instructors alike. Through its accurate data collection and insightful reporting capabilities, the Library E-Gate project sets a new standard for modern library management technology, optimizing processes and facilitating informed decision-making within the academic community.
